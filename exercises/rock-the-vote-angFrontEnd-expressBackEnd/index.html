Veterans heading to Dakota Access Pipeline to protect protesters

Thousands of military veterans are descending on North Dakota to take part in the Standing Rock protest against the planned oil pipeline. One of the veterans we spoke with said, "We fought to defend our countrymen's rights- we wont stand idly by while those rights are infringed upon". This is expected to have a serious impact on the year long protest and occupation because it will be career suicide to be seen using force against America's veterans.
